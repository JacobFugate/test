Evolution of Electric Engines Timeline

  • Invention of the Electric Motor

    The invention of the electric motor marked the beginning of electric propulsion systems, paving the way for various applications in industry and transportation.

    Date: 1834

  • Development of Electric Vehicles

    The development of electric vehicles gained momentum in the late 19th century, with notable advancements in battery technology and motor efficiency.

    Date: Late 19th century

  • Introduction of Regenerative Braking

    Regenerative braking technology, which converts kinetic energy into electrical energy during braking, significantly improves the efficiency of electric vehicles.

    Date: 1960s

  • Rise of Hybrid Electric Vehicles

    The emergence of hybrid electric vehicles combines internal combustion engines with electric propulsion, offering improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.

    Date: 1990s

  • Advancements in Electric Aircraft

    Recent advancements in battery technology and electric propulsion systems have led to the development of electric aircraft, promising eco-friendly aviation solutions.

    Date: 21st century
