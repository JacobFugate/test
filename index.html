Development Timeline of Electric Engines

Milestones

First Electric Motor Invented

The first electric motor was invented by Michael Faraday in 1821, laying the foundation for electric engines.

Development of Alternating Current (AC)

Nikola Tesla's development of alternating current in the late 19th century greatly contributed to the advancement of electric engines.

Introduction of Brushless DC Motors

Brushless DC motors were introduced, offering higher efficiency and reliability compared to traditional brushed motors.

Advancements in Lithium-ion Battery Technology

Advancements in lithium-ion battery technology led to longer battery life and higher energy density, making electric engines more practical.

Introduction of Electric Vehicles

The introduction of electric vehicles marked a significant milestone in the widespread adoption of electric engines for transportation.
